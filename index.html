Flight Price Prediction

In an era where flight ticket prices can be as unpredictable as the weather, harnessing the power of machine learning becomes essential. This project was undertaken to predict future flight prices, providing a valuable tool for both travellers and airlines to make informed decisions. The analysis involved evaluating multiple regression models and implementing hyperparameter tuning to optimize predictive accuracy.

Flight ticket prices are notorious for their volatility, making it challenging for travellers and airlines alike to anticipate and manage costs effectively. The primary objective of this project is to develop a predictive model that can forecast flight prices accurately. Such a model can aid airlines in adjusting pricing strategies, thus optimizing revenue while helping travellers make informed choices.
